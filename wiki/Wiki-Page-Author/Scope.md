# Scope

The skill handles three distinct regions of a page, each with its own rule set:

- The page **body** — everything outside `> [!ODD]` and `> [!CAUTION]` callout blocks. See [Body Rules](Body-Rules).
- `> [!ODD]` callout blocks — section-level uncertainty; rules are inverted from the body. See [Open Design Decision](Open-Design-Decision).
- `> [!CAUTION]` callout blocks — page-level investigation marker; one per page, sits at the top. See [Page Investigation Caution](Page-Investigation-Caution).
